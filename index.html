CORONAVIRUS COVID - 19 SYMPTOMS

  • Sore throat
  • Dry cough
  • Difficulty in breathing
  • High fever

HOW IT SPREADS

  • Air by cough or sneeze
  • Personal contact
  • Contaminated objects
  • Mass gathering

PREVENTION

  • Wash your hands often
  • Wear a face mask
  • Avoid contact with sick people
  • Always cover Your cough or sneeze

FAQs

Q1. Where can I register for COVID-19 vaccination?

You can log into the Co-WIN portal using the link www.cowin.gov.in and click on the “Register/Sign In yourself” tab to register for COVID-19 vaccination.

Q2. Which documents should I carry with me for vaccination?

You should carry your identity proof specified by you at the time of registration on the Co-WIN portal and a printout/screenshot of your appointment slip.

Q3. Whom do I contact in case of side effects from vaccination?

You can contact on any of the following details: Helpline Number: +91-11-23978046 (Toll free- 1075) Helpline Email Id: support@cowin.gov.in
